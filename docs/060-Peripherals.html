Peripherals

Peripheral: a piece of equipment that can be connected to the microcontroller or to the microcontroller board.

Sensors

  • Pressure
  • Temperature
  • Light level
  • Magnetic field
  • Gas flow
  • Tilt
  • Acceleration
  • Contact
  • etc.

Actuators

  • Relay
  • Motor
  • Stepper motor
  • Servomotor
  • etc.

Other peripherals

  • Printer
  • Display
  • OBD connector (On-Board Diagnostics)
  • RFID tag reader
  • PC (!)
  • etc.

Reminder: a peripheral is connected to the MCU or to the CPU by an interface.

But an interface can be seen as a peripheral, depending on the point of view.
